About Global Short Video Trends

Independent analysis of short video trends across China, Europe, and the United States.

Who We Are

Global Short Video Trends is an independent trend monitoring platform tracking short video content patterns across TikTok, Douyin, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and Kuaishou. We analyze creator strategies, emerging formats, and audience engagement data across three major markets.

Our Mission

The short video ecosystem moves fast. Our mission is to provide reliable, data-informed trend analysis that helps creators, marketers, and platform strategists make better decisions. We prioritize accuracy over speed and context over clickbait.

Editorial Standards

Every trend report goes through a multi-step editorial process: data collection from platform APIs and creator analytics tools, cross-referencing against multiple sources, human analysis and interpretation, and editorial review before publication. Statistics are cited with their original sources.
