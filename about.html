About TrendToker

What We Do

TrendToker is an independent news analysis website covering TikTok global trends. We provide daily data-driven trend reports — breaking down viral videos, analyzing niche trends, tracking trending sounds, and reporting on TikTok Shop commerce. Our mission is to help creators, marketers, and brands make informed decisions in the fast-moving world of short-form video.

Our Content Principles

100% data-driven analysis. We do not create trends — we report on them. All data cited in our articles comes from public TikTok Creator Center information or publicly available third-party analytics tools. We do not host, store, or distribute any copyrighted media files.

Daily Coverage

7-8 articles per day across 4 core categories: Viral Top (3 articles), Niche Trend Radar (2 articles), Viral Audio Library (1-2 articles), E-Commerce Boom (1-2 articles). Every article follows a standard four-section format: what happened, data support, deep analysis, actionable takeaways.
